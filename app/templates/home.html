Welcome to Image Classification

This service provides output scores for popular deep learning models from the Torchvision Model Zoo. Beyond classification, our platform offers powerful tools to enhance and analyze your images. With this application, you can adjust image parameters, generate histograms, and download processed results in JPEG or JSON formats.

Site Features

  • Model Classification: Get output scores from popular models in the Torchvision Model Zoo.
  • Image Editing: Adjust image parameters such as color, brightness, contrast, and sharpness before processing.
  • Histogram Calculation: Generate histograms to analyze the distribution of pixel intensity values in RGB channels.
  • Image Upload & Processing: Upload custom images for classification and enhancement.
  • Download Results: Save processed images and histograms in JPEG or JSON format.
  • Model & Image Selection: Choose from a variety of preloaded models and sample images.
